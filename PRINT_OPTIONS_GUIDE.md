# 📄 Print Options Guide

## ✅ New Feature: Customizable Print Preview & PDF Generation

You now have **full control** over what appears in your printed catalogue and PDF exports!

---

## 🎛️ How to Use Print Options

### Step 1: Access Print View

1. **Go to:** https://orville-estate-collection.netlify.app/admin
2. **Login** with your password
3. **Click:** "Print View" button in the header

### Step 2: Configure Print Options

You'll see a **blue panel** at the top with checkboxes:

```
Print Options
☑ Include Complete Price List Page
☑ Show Category Subtotals
☑ Show Grand Total
```

### Step 3: Choose Your Options

**Check or uncheck the boxes** based on what you want:

---

## 📊 Print Options Explained

### Option 1: Include Complete Price List Page

**When CHECKED ✅:**
- Catalogue includes a "Complete Price List" page
- Shows all products organized by category
- Displays Item #, Description, Price, Quantity, Status

**When UNCHECKED ❌:**
- Price list page is completely removed
- Catalogue ends with "Part II Teaser" page
- Only product cards are shown (no price list)

**Use this when:**
- ✅ You want to share with potential buyers (include prices)
- ❌ You want to show just photos without pricing details

---

### Option 2: Show Category Subtotals

**When CHECKED ✅:**
- Each category shows: "X items • Total: $XXX"
- Subtotal appears in category header

**When UNCHECKED ❌:**
- Each category shows only: "X items"
- No subtotal amount displayed

**Use this when:**
- ✅ You want to show value per category
- ❌ You want to hide category totals but still show the list

**Note:** This option only appears when "Include Complete Price List Page" is checked

---

### Option 3: Show Grand Total

**When CHECKED ✅:**
- Shows grand total section at bottom of price list
- Displays: "Total Value: $XXX,XXX"
- Shows total items count

**When UNCHECKED ❌:**
- Grand total section is hidden
- Price list ends after last category

**Use this when:**
- ✅ You want to emphasize total collection value
- ❌ You prefer not to show overall value

**Note:** This option only appears when "Include Complete Price List Page" is checked

---

## 🎯 Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Full Price List (Everything)
```
☑ Include Complete Price List Page
☑ Show Category Subtotals  
☑ Show Grand Total
```
**Result:** Complete catalogue with all pricing information

**Best for:** 
- Official sales catalogue
- Estate sale documentation
- Complete inventory with values

---

### Scenario 2: Price List Without Totals
```
☑ Include Complete Price List Page
☐ Show Category Subtotals
☐ Show Grand Total
```
**Result:** Shows all products with individual prices, but no subtotals or grand total

**Best for:**
- When you want individual prices visible
- When you don't want to emphasize total values
- Simple price reference

---

### Scenario 3: No Price List (Photos Only)
```
☐ Include Complete Price List Page
(Other options disabled)
```
**Result:** Catalogue shows only product cards with photos and descriptions

**Best for:**
- Initial preview for interested parties
- Marketing materials
- When you want to discuss prices separately

## 🖨️ Printing & PDF Export

### The checkboxes affect BOTH:

1. **Print Preview** (what you see on screen)
2. **PDF Export** (when you click "Export to PDF")

### To Print or Export:

1. **Configure options** with checkboxes
2. **Preview** the result by scrolling down
3. **To Print:**
   - Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
   - Or use browser's Print function
4. **To Export PDF:**
   - Click "Export to PDF" button
   - Wait for generation
   - PDF downloads with your chosen options

**The checkboxes won't appear in the print/PDF** - they're only for configuration!
